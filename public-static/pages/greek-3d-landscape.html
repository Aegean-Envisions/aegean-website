The Acropolis

Ancient citadel located on a rocky outcrop above Athens. Contains the remains of several ancient buildings including the Parthenon, symbol of classical Greece and democracy.

The Parthenon

Dedicated to Athena, goddess of wisdom and warfare. Built in 447-432 BCE, it represents the pinnacle of Doric architecture and Greek artistic achievement.

Ancient Agora

The birthplace of democracy where citizens gathered to discuss politics, philosophy, and trade. Socrates taught here and it was the center of Athenian public life.

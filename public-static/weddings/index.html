Why Us

A small team, by design.

Most planners are creative; few are precise. Most are local; few work in four languages. We sit at the intersection — and we keep the team small so the principals are always in the room.

No. 01

Four languages, one conversation

French, German, English, Greek — not as marketing copy, but in real working hours. The Swiss-French market is genuinely underserved. So is the bilingual couple whose families need to be talked to in two tongues.

No. 02

Greek family inside

Vendor pricing without the foreigner premium. Officiants who pick up on the first call. Venues that show you the rooms not on the brochure. A network you can't read your way into — only marry into.

No. 03

Swiss precision, Greek warmth

Budgets tracked in spreadsheets, not vibes. Contracts read line-by-line. Guest logistics, transport, dietary, accessibility — handled in writing, ahead of time. The rest of the day is for you.

No. 04

Limited bookings, on purpose

We accept a small number of weddings per season. Each gets the principals, not a junior. If we say yes, you have us — properly — until the last guest leaves the next morning.

The Greece you'll marry in

There is a particular light over the Aegean in late afternoon — gold tipping into the sea, the pines on the cliff turning amber, the stone of the chapel still warm from the day. We don't romanticise Greece. We just make sure your wedding catches it.
